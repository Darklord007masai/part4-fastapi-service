# Responsible Use Guidelines for Churn Intelligence Outputs

## Approved Operational Applications
* **Proactive Resolution Prioritization**: Use high-risk prediction output queues to route customers directly to high-priority customer service support lines.
* **Value Expansion Incentives**: Offer loyalty and reward programs to high-value, high-risk customer segments to encourage product retention.

## Explicitly Out-of-Scope Activities
* **Dynamic Premium Surcharges**: Do not increase prices or fees for users flagged as highly locked-in or at-risk of churning.
* **Automated Account Penalization**: Do not automate customer account suspension or degrade service quality based solely on algorithmic outputs.
* **Margin Minimization Routines**: Avoid spamming broad, flat coupon codes to low-risk customers, as this unnecessarily reduces overall business margins.
